**Issue Type:** Epic
**Summary:** Leave Request Management and Holiday Tracking System
**Priority:** High
**Assignee:** Development Team
**Reporter:** Product Owner
**Labels:** core-feature, leave-management, hr-operations, compliance
**Epic Name:** LEAVE-MGMT
**Components:** Backend, Frontend, Database, Calendar

**Description:**

As an employee, I need to request time off through a streamlined leave management system. As an HR manager, I need to efficiently process leave requests, track holiday entitlements, and ensure compliance with UK employment regulations.

**Business Value:**
- Streamlined leave request process
- Accurate holiday entitlement tracking
- Compliance with UK Working Time Regulations
- Reduced administrative overhead
- Better workforce planning

**Goals:**
- Enable employees to request various types of leave
- Track holiday entitlements and balances
- Provide approval workflow for managers
- Support UK statutory holiday calculations
- Generate leave reports and analytics

**Acceptance Criteria:**

**Functional Requirements:**
1. **Leave Request Creation**
   - Multiple leave types (Annual, Sick, Maternity, Paternity, etc.)
   - Date range selection with validation
   - Reason/description for leave
   - Attachment support for medical certificates
   - Draft and submit functionality

2. **Leave Entitlement Management**
   - Annual leave allowance calculation
   - Carry-over from previous years
   - Accrual tracking (monthly/annual)
   - Leave balance display
   - UK statutory holiday compliance

3. **Approval Workflow**
   - Manager review and approval
   - Rejection with reasons
   - Approval notifications via email
   - Escalation for urgent requests
   - Audit trail of decisions

4. **Leave Calendar and Planning**
   - Company-wide leave calendar
   - Department leave visibility
   - Conflict detection
   - Holiday planning tools
   - Integration with Outlook/Google Calendar

**Non-Functional Requirements:**
1. **Compliance:** UK Working Time Regulations adherence
2. **Accuracy:** Precise leave balance calculations
3. **Performance:** Calendar loading in <2 seconds
4. **Usability:** Intuitive request interface
5. **Integration:** Calendar export capabilities

**Technical Requirements:**
- Database: LeaveRequest model with relationships
- API: RESTful endpoints with business logic
- Frontend: Calendar integration and date pickers
- Validation: Leave policy rule enforcement
- Notifications: Email templates for approvals
- Reporting: Leave analytics and exports

**Dependencies:**
- Employee Management (for entitlements)
- User Management (for permissions)
- Email service for notifications
- Calendar integration APIs

**Risks:**
- Incorrect leave calculations affecting payroll
- Approval bottlenecks during peak periods
- Calendar integration complexity
- Compliance with changing regulations

**Testing Scenarios:**
1. Submit annual leave request
2. Manager approval process
3. Leave balance calculation
4. Calendar conflict detection
5. Sick leave with medical certificate
6. Leave report generation
7. Bulk approval operations

**Definition of Done:**
- Leave request workflow functional
- Entitlement calculations accurate
- Calendar integration working
- UK compliance verified
- Performance requirements met
- Integration tests passing
- User training materials ready
